Results

Task 1: Here is our pseudocode for instructions on how to make kimchi:

  1. Gather ingredients and supplies
  2. Put cabbage in a large bowl
  3. Pour salt and leave for 30 mins/ 1 hour
  4. Pour bowl into a strainer and drain out water
  5. Return cabbage to bowl
  6. Combine garlic, ginger, shallot, and Korean chili flakes in a blender
  7. Run blender
  8. Optional: Cut carrot, radish, and scallion into small pieces
  9. Combine cabbage, paste, and optional vegetables in bowl
  10. Put on gloves
  11. Mix ingredients by hand
  12. Pack the contents of bowl into large jar
  13. Leave 1-2 inches of room at the top of jar
  14. Cover with a lid but do not screw it on tight
  15. Set in a cool and dark place for 3-5 days
  16. Enjoy!

Task 2: Here is our psuedocode for the Google Chrome T-Rex Game

  1. Check user’s internet connection
  2. Load start screen if the user has no internet connection
  3. Start game if user presses space
  4. Jump if player presses space
  5. Duck if player presses down arrow
  6. Increase score
  7. Increase speed every time user’s score reaches an interval of 100
  8. Stop score and display “Game Over” text if user hits an obstacle
